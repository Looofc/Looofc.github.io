About Loof

I treat Minecraft like a sculpture studio and a film set at once — combining large silhouettes, bold shapes, and cinematic lighting for screenshots, trailers, and playable spaces.

Building stories one block at a time

I specialize in:
  • Organics and character statues for hubs, arenas, and adventure maps.
  • Megastructures and citadels that feel imposing but still navigable in survival.
  • Dioramas designed purely for renders, posters, and thumbnails.

Projects usually range between 50–400 hours depending on scale and detail level.

Commissions

I take on a limited number of commissions per month. Whether you need a statue for a spawn, a full lobby, or a cinematic diorama for a campaign, we’ll sculpt something that fits your world and audience.

Tier I

Characters & Statues

Hero statues, bosses, and NPCs designed as landmarks, lobby focuses, or quest givers.

  • One primary character / creature.
  • Base / pedestal or small surrounding environment.
  • Optimized for screenshots and player scale.
Ideal for server spawns and adventure map focal points.
Tier II

Worlds & Megastructures

Full plazas, citadels, towers, or themed areas with paths, views, and gameplay in mind.

  • Central structure + supporting buildings.
  • Basic terrain shaping and scenic framing.
  • Multiple points of interest / vantage shots.
Best for server hubs, survival spawns, or story locations.
Tier III

Dioramas & Cinematic Pieces

Self-contained scenes crafted purely for art, thumbnails, posters, or trailers.

  • Highly detailed composition and lighting.
  • Can include characters, creatures, and props.
  • Perfect for marketing shots and key art.
Great for content creators and promotional campaigns.
How commissioning works
  1. You send references, scale, and rough budget.
  2. I propose ideas, mood, and rough shapes.
  3. We lock in scope + price, then I start building.
  4. You receive world/schematic + a set of clean renders.
